# Frederick Nchang Cho

## Professional Profile
I am a highly motivated and well-published Clinical Laboratory Scientist with extensive experience in clinical diagnostics, laboratory quality management systems, mentorship, and teaching. My work focuses on strengthening laboratory systems, advancing diagnostic science, and improving health outcomes through quality assurance, research, and capacity building in low-resource settings.
